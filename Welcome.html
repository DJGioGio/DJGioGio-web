⬩ WELCOME TO MY SITE

My name is GioGio (A.K.A. DJ GioGio, Matt or Mattia), I'm a 19 year old content creator with ADHD from the land of Pizza, Pasta & Mandolina (Italy).

I often make Roblox renders with Moon Animator 2 and Photoshop (I use DaVinci Resolve when it comes to animations) and I spend most of my free time gaming, I often play games such as CounterStrike 2, Minecraft, Roblox and many other games.

I make multiple types of content such as Tracks, (Basic) 3D Models, Roblox Renders / Animations, Videos (Editing), Graphic Visuals (Such as Album covers, Song visualizers and wallpapers), I make music that is heavily inspired by Kobaryo, Cametek / Camellia and any famous Uptempo Hardcore / Rawtempo producer out there.

I've been making content since I was 9 years old, I originally started with making shitty memes and (awful) art drawn with a (old and cheap) phone or tablet, throught the years I changed the type of content from making random memes to making other types of videos such as Roblox Animations, Visualizers and much more.
